About Our Project

Smart Plan is a software powered by Artificial Intelligence (AI) that provides convenience for architects and civil engineers. It uses pre-trained models to automatically create 3D floor plans from descriptions, saving them time on initial designs. BuildWise, a built-in advisor, helps with data-driven construction planning throughout the project. Smart AI finds the best AI tools for specific tasks, making it easy to use the latest technology. With Augmented Reality (AR), users can walk through their designs virtually. Complex data is turned into clear pictures that help with decision-making. By taking care of repetitive tasks and complex data analysis, Smart Draft lets architects and civil engineers focus on what they do best: being creative and designing great buildings.

  • Immersive Visualization: Experience your floor plans in a whole new way with augmented reality, allowing you to step into your designs and explore every detail as if you were there in person.
  • Real-Time Editing: Make changes on the fly as you interact with your floor plans in AR, enabling instant adjustments and iterations to ensure your design meets your exact specifications.
  • Collaborative Design: Share AR-enhanced floor plans with clients, colleagues, or friends for seamless communication and decision-making in a shared virtual environment.

What does the layout generation feature do?

Analyzes user input, including spatial requirements, building codes, and design preferences and utilizes AI algorithms to generate multiple floorplan layouts based on the input parameters.

Allows users to customize layout options, such as room sizes, configurations, and circulation patterns and provides real-time feedback on design changes and updates layouts accordingly.

The software provides real-time feedback on design changes, allowing users to instantly visualize the impact of their modifications. It dynamically updates layouts in response to these changes, ensuring that users have an accurate representation of the evolving floorplan throughout the design process.

How can Data Visualization be useful for floorplanning?

Efficiency Boost: Data visualization in AI-powered layout software streamlines design processes by converting intricate data into intuitive visuals.

Informed Decisions: Users gain insights into spatial constraints, user preferences, and traffic flow patterns, enabling informed layout optimization.

Enhanced Designs: From interactive heatmaps to 3D renderings, visualizations aid in creating more functional and visually appealing layouts, elevating design outcomes.

Green Buildings for sustenance

Energy Efficiency Analysis: This feature involves a detailed assessment of a building's energy consumption patterns. It includes analyzing the efficiency of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems, as well as recommending upgrades for improved energy efficiency.

Environmental Impact Assessment: This feature focuses on evaluating the environmental footprint of a building or construction project. It includes analyzing factors such as the carbon footprint, water usage, and waste generation. Based on this assessment, the software can recommend environmentally friendly materials and construction practices to minimize environmental impact.

Green Certification Support: This feature streamlines the process of obtaining green building certifications, such as LEED or BREEAM. It helps users track their compliance with certification criteria and generates reports that are required for certification submissions.

Cost Estimation for Contruction

leverages AI algorithms to analyze floorplan designs, material specifications, labor requirements, and market prices to generate accurate cost estimates. Has variables such as room dimensions, building codes, and sustainability criteria to provide detailed cost breakdowns. This feature helps architects, engineers, and project managers make informed decisions, optimize budgets.

Blue-Print to Dimensions

Blueprint dimensions are crucial as they provide precise measurements and specifications for every aspect of a design, ensuring accuracy and consistency during construction or manufacturing. They serve as a detailed guide for architects, engineers, and builders, facilitating clear communication and reducing the risk of errors.
